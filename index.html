About & Why New Dawn?

New Dawn is a platform that allows the public to help the community in need in the best way they can. New Dawn will act as a census for the Homeless Population for the public to access and thus aid.

New Dawn allows public to log into their accounts and search for people in need near them and help them, whether by donation of items, money or a small job. How do we get the data about the homeless? From the users of new dawn! See someone in need at an intersection? Create a post about them, add a picture, a location and a few workds if you wish. And everyone else in the nearby area will be able to see the these posts and go and help the population
